FaceT

Mobile App Development

Poster Demo
Video Demo

Clothes make the man, so does make-up. Being beautiful is a life-long career for all women. With no doubt, they spend numerous time and money on purchasing, searching, as well as testing different new cosmetic products. However, it is common that they are unable to find even one suitable make-up product after they have tried all displays in various stores. This is troublesome, time consuming, and inconvenient. In order to facilitate women’s process in choosing and purchasing appropriate cosmetic products,FaceT is proposed.

The name of the proposed app is FaceT, which is pronounced as Facet. This name is chosen because makeup is believed to be only one of the Facet to a person. In addition, FaceT is combined by the words Face and T. Face is the main theme of the app, whereas T can be extended for meaning Technology. Therefore, FaceT can also be interpreted as Face Technology, explaining that the app is using technology to masquerade the user’s face.

  • Technologies Deployed: UI/UX Design, Android Development, Content Management System, Image Processing, Real-Time Push Notifications, User Behavior Tracking
  • Completed Date: April 2017
  • Type: Final Year Project
  • Remarks: One of the Best FYP (Year 2016-17)
